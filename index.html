ABOUT US

Founded by Nathan Barna and Taylor Baroth, Opimal Evolution was created in response to seeing so many coaches, trainers, therapists, dietitians, and doctors misguiding people and/or not addressing the root causes of an individual's disease, poor health, or diminished performance. Through a holistic approach, we teach people how to create health, happiness, strength, and vitality in their lives so that they have the greatest opportunity to reach their dreams. We treat each individual as a complete, integrated system, recognizing the synergistic effort that all body systems have on one another.

Our practice teaches people to develop a relationship with their body so that they ultimately gain the power to be their own guiding force to the life they want to live. We believe that most ailments occur through the mismanagement of one’s lifestyle choices that can be traced back to a few key principles. And through embracing and optimizing these principles one learns how to create a body that is free of sickness and disease, capable of achieving the highest levels of human potential: physically, mentally, emotionally, and spiritually.

It is our mission is to be a source of light for those that recognize the importance of aligning mind, body, soul, and spirit to create not only a better life for oneself, but to use one’s love and health for the betterment of all life. Change must happen on a personal level before it can happen on global and universal level. By helping yourself first you are putting yourself in a position to help those around you.

Whether your dream is to be an elite athlete or to be the best parent, teacher, husband, or sister you can be, our goal is to guide your through your journey and help you discover your own Optimal Evolution.

Peace, Love, and Strength,
Nathan Barna & Taylor Baroth

Nathan is an Athlete, Strength Coach, and a Licensed Holistic Lifestyle Coach. Nathan’s career began as a competitive strength sport athlete. As an athlete he was always looking for ways to improve his performance. He realized that being the best was much more than just training hard in the gym. To fulfill his dream of being a world class athlete, he had to learn how to optimize every aspect of his life: physically, mentally, emotionally, and spiritually. Through his years of practice, study, successes and failures, he has learned that the same principles that help an athlete succeed at a high level are the same principles that can help every human become a stronger, healthier, happier version of him or herself. Now, alongside fulfilling his potential as a strength athlete, Nathan’s mission is to use his skills to help others achieve their dreams through a practical, scientific, and integrative approach that addresses every human as a holistic stystem, not a machine of separate parts and pieces.

Taylor is a Registered Nurse and a Licensed Holistic Lifestyle Coach. She began her professional career in the Newborn Intensive Care Unit helping to care for critically ill and premature babies. After years of working night shift as a graduate nurse and putting her career first, she realized she needed to incorporate exercise back into her life and start putting herself and her happiness first, which meant getting back into exercise and changing her lifestyle.

Growing up she was an athlete and very involved in sports so being active came very natural to her. A friend connected her with a personal trainer who eventually recommended that she try strength training. She had no idea that a brand new form of exercise would become a sport that sparked a fire within her that she never felt before.
