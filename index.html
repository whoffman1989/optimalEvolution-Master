We have a talent with amazing skill

Nathan Barna, HLC, ACSM-CPT

CEO & Founder

Nathan is an Athlete, Strength Coach, and a Licensed Holistic Lifestyle Coach. Nathan’s career began as a competitive strength sport athlete. As an athlete he was always looking for ways to improve his performance. He realized that being the best was much more than just training hard in the gym. To fulfill his dream of being a world class athlete, he had to learn how to optimize every aspect of his life: physically, mentally, emotionally, and spiritually.

Through his years of practice, study, successes and failures, he has learned that the same principles that help an athlete succeed at a high level are the same principles that can help every human become a stronger, healthier, happier version of him or herself.

Now, alongside fulfilling his potential as a strength athlete, Nathan’s mission is to use his skills to help others achieve their dreams through a practical, scientific, and integrative approach that addresses every human as a holistic stystem, not a machine of separate parts and pieces.

Nathan Barna, RN, HLC

CEO & Founder

Taylor is a Registered Nurse and a Licensed Holistic Lifestyle Coach. She began her professional career in the Newborn Intensive Care Unit helping to care for critically ill and premature babies. After 3 years of working night shift as a graduate nurse and putting her career first, she realized she needed to start putting herself and her happiness first, which meant changing her lifestyle and getting back into exercise.

Growing up she was an athlete and very involved in several sports so being active came very natural to her. A friend connected her with a personal trainer who eventually recommended that she try strength training. She had no idea that a brand new form of exercise would become a sport that sparked a fire within her that she never felt before.

Through weightlifting, she started to develop a healthy relationship with food and learned the importance of fueling her body in ways that facilitate the best performance. Not only did she start making progress in the gym but she was also feeling more in control of her mental and emotional state as well. She was becoming more in tune with her body and learned to pay attention to what her body was telling her with each choice she made. This allowed her to love and trust her body for all it was capable of instead of only equating her worth with her outward appearance or a number on the scale.

In order for her to continue to implement a healthy lifestyle, she realized that she needed to come off of night shift which ultimately led her to transition from working with babies to accepting a new position. She continued to follow what her heart was telling her and began studying holistic health. Through her studies, she has developed an understanding of what optimal health looks like. With the knowledge she has, it has become more apparent to her that her duty in serving others as a nurse is not being fulfilled in the way that she feels people deserve or in a way that fulfills her own heart.

Taylor's very own journey and continuous education has helped her discover that we all have magic inside of us that sometimes needs a little help finding. She is dedicated to helping others learn to love, trust, and believe in themselves in order to actualize their potential and accomplish their goals and dreams.

ABOUT US

Founded by Nathan Barna and Taylor Baroth, Opimal Evolution was created in response to seeing so many coaches, trainers, therapists, dietitians, and doctors misguiding people and/or not addressing the root causes of an individual's disease, poor health, or diminished performance. Through a holistic approach, we teach people how to create health, happiness, strength, and vitality in their lives so that they have the greatest opportunity to reach their dreams. We treat each individual as a complete, integrated system, recognizing the synergistic effort that all body systems have on one another.

Our practice teaches people to develop a relationship with their body so that they ultimately gain the power to be their own guiding force to the life they want to live. We believe that most ailments occur through the mismanagement of one’s lifestyle choices that can be traced back to a few key principles. And through embracing and optimizing these principles one learns how to create a body that is free of sickness and disease, capable of achieving the highest levels of human potential: physically, mentally, emotionally, and spiritually.

It is our mission is to be a source of light for those that recognize the importance of aligning mind, body, soul, and spirit to create not only a better life for oneself, but to use one’s love and health for the betterment of all life. Change must happen on a personal level before it can happen on global and universal level. By helping yourself first you are putting yourself in a position to help those around you.

Whether your dream is to be an elite athlete or to be the best parent, teacher, husband, or sister you can be, our goal is to guide your through your journey and help you discover your own Optimal Evolution.
